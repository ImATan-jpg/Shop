ABOUT AURELUX

AURELUX was founded in 2026 with one goal. Build garments that hold presence without excess. We focus on structure, fabric integrity, and discipline in design. Each piece reflects patience, precision, and modern restraint.

Our atelier merges classic tailoring with contemporary culture. The result is clothing that feels timeless yet current.
